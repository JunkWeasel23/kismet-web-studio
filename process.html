Process

Reach Out:

  • Send me an email with your ideas, inspiration, references, sketches, or examples -- even if it's messy.
  • You don't need everything figured out yet. I'll help you shape it.

Quote & Scope:

  • Based on your needs, I'll recommend a structure (individual pages, add-ons, or a package) and send a clear quote.
  • We'll make sure the scope and pricing feel right before moving forward.

Deposit & Scheduling:

  • Once we're aligned, you can choose to pay:
  • 50% upfront / 50% on completion OR
  • 100% upfront
  • Your project is officially scheduled once payment is received.

Build Phase:

  • I design and hand-code your website from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript -- no templates, no page builders.
  • Most small sites are completed within 5-14 days, depending on scope.
  • I also maintain internal versioned backups throughout the build to keep everything organized and secure.

Review & Revisions:

  • You'll receive a full draft of your site to review.
  • From that point, you'll have a 1-week revision window for minor changes and refinements included in the project.

Launch or Handoff:

  • Once revisions are complete:
  • I can help you publish the site OR
  • Deliver the final files if you prefer to host elsewhere
  • Optional source-code ownership is available for clients who want full long-term flexibility.

Future Updates:

  • Need changes later?
  • New pages, content updates, or feature additions can always be added when you're ready.

Priority Turnaround (optional):

  • Need your site sooner?
  • Priority projects (+20-50% of total price) jump to the front of the schedule for faster delivery (availability varies).
